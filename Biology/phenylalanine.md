## Notes
**Phenylalanine (Phe)** $\ce{C9H11NO2}$ is an [[amino acid]] with a [[benzyl]] or [[phenyl]] side chain. This can be thought of replacing the terminal [[hydrogen]] atom in [[alanine]] with a [[phenyl]] group.
