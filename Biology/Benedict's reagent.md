## Notes
**Benedict's reagent** detects [[aldehyde|aldehydes]] and [[ketone|ketones]]. It contains [[sodium]] [[carbonate]] $\ce{Na2CO3}$, sodium citrate, and [[copper]] [[sulfate]] penta[[hydrate]] $\ce{CuSO4*5H2O}$.
