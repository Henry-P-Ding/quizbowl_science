## Notes
One definition of $\cosh x$ (**hyperbolic cosine**) is:
$$
\cosh x = \frac{e^{ix} + e^{-ix}}{2}
$$
It is one example of a [[hyperbolic trigonometric function]].

---
The **catenary**, the shape of a hanging rope, is in the form of $\cosh x$.
