## Notes
**Proton decay** is hypothetical decay in which a [[proton]] decays. One example is:
$$
\ce{p+ -> e+ + \pi 0 -> 2\gamma}
$$
Modern experiments have placed the half-life of proton decay to be at least $10^{34 \sim 36}$ years.
