### Metal Aquo Complex
**Metal aquo complexes** are coordination complexes containing a metal and only water as a [[ligand|ligands]]. Most dissolved metal [[salts]] form metal aquo complexes. Common metal aquo complexes include:
- $\ce{[Cu(H2O)6]^{2+}}$
