## Notes
**Delta-v** in rocket physics is the [[impulse]] per unit [[mass]] required for a rocket maneuver. It is defined as:
$$\Delta v = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} \frac{|F(t)|}{m(t)}\diff t$$
